About us

A young man, Manu von Franquemont, was incarnated by the god Manuhmet al Cantaloupe. This allowed Manuhmet to spread the Melonian faith. Manuhmet is the most discussed god out of all Melonian Gods. This is because he is the Chosen One.

The Melon is a religion unlike any other; we have no rules or expectations as long as you vote for our political party:
the Melonian Party.

The Melon stands for 3 things:

Economic Freedom

No more economic crisises... can you imagine that? Well, we can; we can even make it happen.

A world with none of the current currencies. No Euro, no Dollar, no Peso, only Hanaar; a currency you get just by living!

Individual Financial Independence

Because of our currency 'the Hanaar', you'll have financial independence. You work for your needs, this can be done in many ways. As long as you work, you'll be good in terms of necessities of life and that way you have enough Hanaar for luxury goods.

Equality

No-one will be judged by their gender, race, age, disability or sexuality.

Some exceptions are: furry's (sexuality), religion and opinion.
